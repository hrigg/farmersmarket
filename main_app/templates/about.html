About Farmer's Market Finder

Farmer's Market Finder is a way to connect with local vendors

Started in 2022, Finder's Market was created as a place for vendors to put their shop information connected to the Farmer's Markets they attend. We create a space for vendors to connect with other markets and vendors and for users to see all of the Markets and Vendors in their area.


Reach out to us if you have a market or vendor you want to add to our site!
